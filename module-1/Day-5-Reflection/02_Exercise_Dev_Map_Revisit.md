# Exercise: Personal Development Map Revisit
**Time: 60 Minutes**

## Overview
This is the most critical assessment activity of the week. Has your perspective shifted after four intensive days? This exercise demonstrates whether the Day 1 frameworks actually landed and sets an accurate baseline for the remaining 9 weeks.

## Instructions
Revisit your Day 1 Personal Development Map.

### Part 1: Self-Calibration
1.  **Re-rate:** Would you change any of your 1-5 self-ratings on the Five Mindsets? Why?
2.  **Gap Analysis:** What new gaps did you discover over the week? (Common examples: Realizing your CI/CD knowledge is weaker than expected after the One-Day Ship, or that your agent security knowledge is lacking after the Nanobot Deep Dive).
3.  **Refine Intention:** Based on this week's experience, refine your "10-week intention". Make it more specific. Instead of "I want to learn CI/CD", write "I want to build a reusable pipeline template for all my future projects."

### Part 2: Peer Review
Pair up with your partner from Day 1. Share your updated maps. Compare your Day 1 ratings versus Day 5 ratings and discuss the differences.

*(Instructors will circulate for short 1:1 progress checks).*
