# Exercise: NotebookLM Mastery
**Time: 90 Minutes**

## Overview
NotebookLM is your personalized, AI-powered study companion for the next 10 weeks. Its 1M token context window allows you to build a comprehensive, grounded knowledge base of all course materials.

## Part 1: Your Study Notebook
Create your "Renaissance Developer Study Notebook" in NotebookLM.

1.  Upload the main curriculum outline PDF.
2.  Upload any Module 2 pre-readings or reference articles provided by your instructors.
3.  Upload your own notes and completed exercises from this week.

## Part 2: Audio Overview
Generate an Audio Overview (Deep Dive format) focusing on the Module 2 material. 
*   **Action:** Listen to this audio on your commute or over the weekend.

## Part 3: Flashcards & Quizzes
1.  **Self-Assessment:** Ask NotebookLM to generate a 10-question self-assessment quiz on Week 1 material (Git, AI Tools, Agent Architecture). Take the quiz.
2.  **Socratic Review:** For any questions you answer incorrectly or feel uncertain about, use the "Explain" prompt to have the AI guide you through the concept Socratically.
3.  **Flashcards:** Auto-generate flashcards for key terms (e.g., The Five Mindsets, Nanobot, Spec-Driven Development) and export them to Anki or Quizlet if you use them.

## Part 4: Mind Map
Create a mind map (on paper, in a Miro board, or using markdown) mapping how the five AI tools we've explored relate to the four competency layers of the Renaissance Developer. Let NotebookLM assist you in outlining this connection if you get stuck.
