# Guide: Retrospective & Homework (One-Day Ship)
**Time: 30 Minutes**

## Overview
The retrospective is a core component of the true Renaissance Developer's **Process** responsibility. We measure our cycle times and learn from our mistakes to deliver more rapidly while maintaining quality.

## Sailboat Retrospective (Structured)
Reflect on the build today:
1.  **What went well?** (The Wind)
2.  **What was much harder than expected?** (The Anchor)
3.  **What would you change about your process next time?** (The Rocks)

**Key Discussion Question:** 
How did relying on AI tools alter your approach versus building an app completely manually without them?

## Homework: Blog Post
Write a short blog post (300-500 words) in your portfolio repository wiki documenting your build. 

**Title:** "My First AI-Augmented Build — What I Learned"

Reflect on your experience today, specifically addressing the difference between generating code and verifying it. This exercises your **Technical Writing** competency (Layer 3: Enabler).
