# Prompt Engineering Foundations
Systematic prompt engineering — not ad hoc, but repeatable patterns.

1. **Context Setting:** Giving AI enough background without overwhelming.
2. **Constraint Specification:** Language, framework, testing requirements, style guidelines.
3. **Iterative Refinement:** Narrowing scope based on initial output.
4. **Chain-of-Thought Prompting:** Asking AI to explain its reasoning before coding.
