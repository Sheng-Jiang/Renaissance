# Exercise: Personal Development Map
**Time: 90 Minutes**

## Overview
Using the frameworks introduced this morning, you will create a structured self-assessment. This is a vulnerability and calibration exercise to identify your starting point and the transformation you seek over the next 10 weeks. This map will be a living document that we revisit at the end of every module.

## Instructions
Complete the following four parts to build your map. 

### Part A: The DNA Radar Chart
Rate yourself on each of the **Five Core Mindsets** from 1 to 5.
*(1 = Needs significant development, 3 = Competent/Proficient, 5 = Exceptional/Role Model).*

1. **Curiosity (保持好奇):** Do I discover problems or just solve tickets? 
2. **Outcome (结果导向):** Am I accountable for business impact or just code?
3. **Polymath (博学思维):** Do I translate specialized knowledge and think like a business insider?
4. **Precision (精准沟通):** Do I bridge the gap between executives and workers with clarity?
5. **Systems (系统思考):** Can I create order from chaos?

*Self-Reflection:*
- Where are you strongest? Provide one brief career example as evidence.
- Where is your biggest gap?

### Part B: The Competency Layers
For each of the four competency layers, list your existing strong skills and identify your gaps.

* **Layer 1: Technical Foundation:** Cloud, Data Modeling, DevOps/CI/CD, Lean Thinking, Tech Debt.
* **Layer 2: Architect:** Architecture & Design, Code Quality, SRE, Service Management.
* **Layer 3: Enabler:** Pattern Generalization, Knowledge Management, Technical Writing, Developer Experience.
* **Layer 4: Strategist & Leader:** Business Immersion, Data Integration, Multi-Audience Communication, Problem Discovery.

### Part C: The Strategist Venn Diagram
Where do you naturally fall on the spectrum between **Business Strategy** and **Technical Execution**?
* Plot yourself on this spectrum.
* Are you heavily skewed? Do you bridge the gap effectively? Where is the overlap strongest for you?

### Part D: Your 10-Week Intention
Write a specific intention. What transformation do you want by graduation? Example: *"I want to stop being afraid of CI/CD pipelines and become the person who designs them."*
