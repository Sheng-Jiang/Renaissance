# Exercise: Cohort Norms
**Time: 30 Minutes**

## Overview
The "operating agreement" for our cohort. Since the Renaissance Developer operates on principles of clarity and teamwork (Delivery, People, Process), we need explicitly stated norms for how we will collaborate over the next 10 weeks.

## Instructions
1.  **Facilitated Discussion:** What agreements should we make about how we work together?
2.  **Examples:**
    *   "Review PRs within 4 hours."
    *   "Ask for help before being stuck for 30 minutes."
    *   "Demos over slide decks."
    *   "No defensive code reviews—assume positive intent."
3.  **Documentation:** As a cohort, agree on the top 5-7 norms. We will document these in the shared GitHub repository as our operating agreement.
