# The Executive Presentation Format

Every student presents: **5 minutes, strictly timed.**

1.  **The Problem (1 min):** User pain, supported by discovery quotes.
2.  **The Opportunity (1 min):** Market context, gap, your unique insight.
3.  **The Proposal (1 min):** What you'll build, for whom, with what expected impact.
4.  **The Ask (1 min):** Investment required, ROI, timeline, risks.
5.  **Why Me (1 min):** Why you are the right person to build this.

**Key rule:** NO live demo, NO technical architecture slides. This is a **business** presentation.

---

# Judging Criteria

The audience plays the role of an investment committee:

| Criterion | What They're Looking For |
|---|---|
| **Problem Clarity** | Is the pain real and well-evidenced? |
| **Solution Viability** | Is the proposal realistic for 2 weeks? |
| **Business Impact** | Are the ROI numbers credible? |
| **Customer Evidence** | Was discovery rigorous? Findings convincing? |
| **Presentation Quality** | Clear, concise, and executive-appropriate? |
