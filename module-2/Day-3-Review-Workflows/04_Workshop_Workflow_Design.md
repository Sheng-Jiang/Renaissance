# Workshop: Team Workflow Design
**Time: 75 Minutes**

## Overview
This is the capstone synthesis for Day 3. You will design an end-to-end framework for a small engineering team using AI tools safely and effectively.

## Instructions
Form groups of 3–4. Design a complete workflow for a theoretical team of 5 engineers using AI code generation tools.

Address the following questions in your design:
1.  **Roles:** Who generates the code? Who reviews it? How does the review process differ from traditional, human-only reviews?
2.  **Automation:** What is automated in your CI/CD pipeline (e.g., complexity limits, coverage requirements)? What strictly requires human judgment?
3.  **Conflict Resolution:** How do you handle disagreements between the AI's generated approach and your team's established conventions?
4.  **Onboarding:** How do you safely onboard new team members into this highly-automated workflow without them blindly trusting the AI?
5.  **Feedback Loop:** How do you track AI-introduced defects in production and feed those learnings back into your prompt playbooks?

## Presentation
Each group will present their workflow design to the class (5 minutes). 

The class will vote on:
*   Most Practical
*   Most Innovative
*   Most Scalable

*Key question to ask yourself: Does your workflow make the team faster AND more reliable, or does it trade one for the other?*
